About Twins Physiotherapist

At Twins Physiotherapist, our practice is built on a meticulous and comprehensive approach to patient care. We focus on performing an in-depth analysis of your recovery journey to uncover the specific biomechanical root causes of your discomfort, rather than just managing surface-level symptoms. This dedicated focus ensures that every treatment plan is highly optimized and precisely tailored to your physical requirements, lifestyle, and recovery timeline.


Our mission is to empower you with the essential tools and knowledge required to take ownership of your physical well-being, transforming your rehabilitation into a foundation for sustained mobility. At Twins Physiotherapy, we champion the philosophy that Movement is Medicine, ensuring every patient has the support they need to return to their best, most active self.


Our clinic is committed to maintaining the highest standards of clinical excellence by integrating evidence-based practices with modern therapeutic technology. We focus on a results-oriented approach to rehabilitation, utilizing precise diagnostic assessments to monitor your progress and adjust treatment plans with clinical accuracy. This professional methodology ensures that every session contributes directly to your long-term mobility goals, providing you with a clear and efficient pathway back to peak physical performance.

Why Choose Us?

We combine years of clinical expertise with a deeply personal touch. Our philosophy is simple: Movement is Medicine. Whether you are an athlete recovering from a ligament tear, a senior looking to maintain independence, or someone managing chronic back pain, we are committed to getting you back to your best self.


Expert Clinical Care: From Orthopedic and Neurological rehab to Pediatric care, we use evidence-based techniques for faster recovery.
Patient-Centric Care: We take the time to listen and understand the root cause of your pain, creating a roadmap to recovery that fits your life.
Comprehensive Facilities: We offer Manual Therapy, Electrotherapy, and Dry Needling in a professional environment.

Specialized Services

Our Specialized Services

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Relieve Pain. Restore Movement. Regain Strength. We treat joint, muscle, and bone conditions including back/neck pain and sports injuries.

Neurological Physiotherapy

Rebuild Movement. Reclaim Independence. Expert rehab for stroke, Parkinson's, and spinal cord injuries using neuroplasticity training.

Sports Physiotherapy

Return Stronger Than Before. Injury prevention and performance enhancement for athletes of all levels.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Guided Recovery After Surgery. Structured plans to safely regain mobility after joint replacements or spine surgeries.

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Caring for Growing Bodies. Play-based techniques to help children with developmental delays or cerebral palsy.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Healthy Aging. Active Living. Improving balance and strength for seniors to maintain independence and prevent falls.

Pain Management Therapy

Target the Root Cause. Advanced techniques like dry needling and posture correction to manage chronic pain.

Manual Therapy

Hands-On Healing. Specialized joint mobilization and soft tissue release to improve mobility and reduce pain.

Electrotherapy

Technology-Assisted Recovery. Using ultrasound, laser, and TENS to accelerate healing and reduce inflammation.

Women's Health

Specialized Care for Women. Treatment for postnatal conditions, pelvic floor dysfunction, and C-section rehab.

Cardio-Respiratory

Breathe Better. Live Better. Rehab for asthma, COPD, and post-cardiac surgery using pulmonary programs.

Ergonomic & Postural

Correct Your Posture. Workstation assessments and corrective exercises for desk workers and students.

Home Visit Physiotherapy

Care at Your Doorstep. Professional services for patients who cannot travel, including post-stroke and elderly care.

Fitness & Rehabilitation

Train Smart. Stay Strong. Customized fitness programs focused on strength, flexibility, and injury prevention.

Advanced Pain Relief

Modern Therapeutic Techniques. Effective treatment for muscle tightness and trigger points using dry needling.
